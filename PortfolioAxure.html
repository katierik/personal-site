Prototyping using Axure

Axure is one of my favorite prototyping tools. I love the ability to do complex interactions and relatively high fidelity prototypes all in the same application. Although I've got my own Axure pet peeves, overall this is one of my preferred tools

This particular design for editing details for a personal auto policy while the policy is currently in force is meant to plug into different applications and be used for a couple different users. Users could be the customer themselves, coming through to access this through a self-servive portal; or a customer service rep updating information while speaking to the customer on the phone. For this reason, a relatively simple structure was used with the navigation contained inside the key screens, so that this widget could be dropped into a variety of portals.
